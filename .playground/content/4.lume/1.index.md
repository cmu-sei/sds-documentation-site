# Lume

*Enhance, design, and export beautiful visuals—right in your workflow.*

**Lume** is a lightweight, browser-based image enhancement and design tool built for speed, simplicity, and visual consistency. Whether you're editing a single image or designing an entire set of visual assets, Lume provides an intuitive interface and powerful controls to help you work faster without compromising quality.

From basic adjustments like cropping and contrast to layered design templates and export-ready assets, Lume is ideal for creating visuals for presentations, social media, internal documentation, and more. With built-in support for high-DPI rendering and accessible color contrast suggestions, your visuals don’t just look great—they’re also inclusive and on-brand.
