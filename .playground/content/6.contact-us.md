# Contact Us

We’re here to help with any questions, issues, or ideas you have about the **SEI Design System (SDS)**. Whether you’re looking for guidance, want to report a bug, or have a feature request, there are several ways to reach the team.

---

## 💬 Mattermost

Join our channel on Mattermost for real-time discussion and community support. Use it to:

* Ask questions about using this template
* Share feedback or suggestions
* Connect with other designers and developers
* Stay informed about new releases and updates

👉 [Join the SEI Design System channel](https://chatops.sei.cmu.edu/sei/channels/sei-design-system-public)

---

## 🐞 Report a Bug

If you’ve found something that’s not working as expected, please let us know so we can fix it.

👉 [Report a Bug](https://designsystem.sei.cmu.edu/help/report-a-bug)

---

## ✨ Request a Feature

Have an idea that could make SDS or this template even better? We’d love to hear it.

👉 [Request a New Feature](https://designsystem.sei.cmu.edu/help/request-a-new-feature)

---

## 📧 Email Us

You can also reach the SDS team directly by email at
**[sei-design-system@sei.cmu.edu](mailto:sei-design-system@sei.cmu.edu)**. We’ll do our best to respond within a few business days.
