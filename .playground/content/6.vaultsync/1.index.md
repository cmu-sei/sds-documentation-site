# VaultSync

*Secure collaboration, simplified.*

**VaultSync** is a cloud-based file collaboration and backup system designed to help teams securely store, share, and sync files across devices and platforms. With end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, and seamless integrations, VaultSync ensures your data is protected while remaining easily accessible to the people who need it.

Whether you're working across teams, managing sensitive documents, or coordinating deliverables with external partners, VaultSync gives you the tools to collaborate confidently—without sacrificing security or control.
