# Getting Started with VaultSync

Welcome to VaultSync! This quick-start guide walks you through setting up your workspace, uploading your first files, and sharing content securely.

If you haven’t already, be sure to review the [Installation Guide](../installation-guide) to install or access VaultSync in your environment.

---

## ✅ Step 1: Sign In or Create an Account

Visit [vaultsync.app](https://vaultsync.app) and either:

- Sign in using your organization’s credentials  
- Or create a new workspace with your email and a secure password

If your team uses SSO (Single Sign-On), select the appropriate provider on the login screen.

---

## 🏢 Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

Once logged in, you'll be prompted to:

1. **Name your workspace** (e.g., “Acme Product Team”)  
2. **Invite team members** via email or Slack  
3. **Configure your default permissions** (e.g., all files private by default, or shared with editors)

You can always update these settings later in **Workspace Settings**.

---

## 📁 Step 3: Upload Your First Files

You can upload files by:

- Dragging and dropping them into the browser  
- Using the “New Upload” button in the top-right corner  
- Syncing from connected services (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)

Files are automatically encrypted and stored in your workspace's cloud storage.

---

## 🔗 Step 4: Share Securely

To share a file or folder:

1. Click the “Share” icon next to the item  
2. Choose recipients and set access level (view, comment, or edit)  
3. Optionally set an expiration date, password, or single-use restriction  
4. Send a direct link or generate a public link if enabled

💡 You can also share with external users without requiring them to sign up.

---

## 🛡️ Step 5: Manage Access & Permissions

Under **Settings > Permissions**, you can:

- Assign roles like Admin, Editor, or Viewer  
- Set custom permissions per file or folder  
- View who has access and revoke at any time  
- Enable workspace-level compliance settings (available in version 1.2.0+)

---

## 🖥️ Optional: Download the Desktop or Mobile App

To sync files across devices, install VaultSync for:

- **Desktop** (Windows/macOS) – Available in your dashboard  
- **Mobile** (iOS & Android) – Available in the App Store and Google Play
