# Features

FlowStack is built to simplify how teams automate, manage, and optimize workflows—without writing code. Below are its core features designed to help you move faster with less friction.

---

## Drag-and-Drop Workflow Builder

Design powerful workflows with a visual, intuitive interface.

- Create complex automations using a node-based canvas  
- Add triggers, actions, conditions, and branches with ease  
- Rearrange steps visually—no scripting required  
- View dependencies and execution flow at a glance  
- Ideal for onboarding new users or collaborating across teams

---
  
## Pre-Built Automation Templates

Get started quickly with ready-made workflows for common use cases.

- Choose from a library of curated templates:  
  - Email notifications  
  - Task assignment and reminders  
  - Daily summary digests  
  - Form submission handlers  
- Customize templates to match your exact logic  
- Great for prototyping or operationalizing existing manual processes  
- Save and share your own templates across teams

---

## Integration with Third-Party Apps

Connect your tools and make your workflows smarter.

- Native support for popular platforms like:  
  - Slack, Microsoft Teams, and email  
  - Google Calendar and Trello  
  - GitHub, Jira, and Notion  
- Use webhooks and API endpoints for custom integrations  
- Automate across systems to eliminate repetitive switching  
- Trigger workflows based on external events or updates
