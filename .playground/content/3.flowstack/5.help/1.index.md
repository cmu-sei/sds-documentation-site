## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why isn’t my workflow triggering?**  
A: Make sure the trigger conditions are correctly configured, and that any external apps are connected and authorized.

**Q: Can I test a workflow before enabling it?**  
A: Yes. Use the “Test Run” feature to simulate execution and inspect outputs step-by-step.

**Q: How do I view execution history?**  
A: Navigate to **Workflows > Execution Logs**. You can filter by status, time, or workflow name.

**Q: What happens if a step fails?**  
A: You’ll see a detailed error log in the workflow history. If enabled, notifications will be sent to designated users or channels.

## Contact Support

If you can’t find what you need in the docs, our support team is here to help.

**Ways to reach us:**

- 📧 **Email**: [support@flowstack.app](mailto:support@flowstack.app)  
- 💬 **Slack** (internal users): #flowstack-help  
- 🐞 **Report a Bug**: [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/your-org/flowstack/issues)

When contacting support, please include:

- Workflow name or ID  
- Time of issue  
- Any error messages or screenshots (if applicable)
