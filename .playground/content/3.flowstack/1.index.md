FlowStack is a workflow automation and task orchestration tool designed to help teams streamline repetitive processes, improve efficiency, and reduce manual effort. Whether you're automating email notifications, managing recurring tasks, or integrating with third-party tools, FlowStack provides a visual interface and intelligent suggestions to help you build, monitor, and optimize workflows with ease.

With built-in support for drag-and-drop logic, pre-built templates, smart triggers, and external app integrations, FlowStack empowers both technical and non-technical users to design powerful automations—without writing a single line of code.
